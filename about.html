Our Story

We’re Katie and Rachel — two athletes from Scotland who accidentally became a beach volleyball team and haven’t looked back since.

We train in the cold, and squeeze in gym sessions between, studying, and working. But we love this sport and we’re proud to be representing Scotland wherever we go.

We’re not full-time athletes (yet), but we’ve beaten full-time international teams. We’ve earned spots on podiums, in qualification rounds. Now we’re aiming even higher — and we’d love to take you with us.
